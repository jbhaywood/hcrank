Choose your card

Remaining:

 

Here's how this works...

Choose a hero and then pick the best card out of the three every time after that, just like an arena draft. But, instead of trying to build a deck, pretend that every choice is the first pick. This is not about making a viable deck, it's just about picking the best cards.

After 30 picks, you can see how well you did. Keep trying to improve your score so that when you do a real arena run you'll be able to quickly judge the cards you're presented with and make the best deck you can.

 
 
Remember, you're not making a deck. Just pick the best card of the three.
